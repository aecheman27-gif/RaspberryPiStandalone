Lesson Schematics

One page per lesson — proper schematics + the exact breadboard hole for each connection
How to read these
  • Symbols: zig-zag = resistor · triangle+bar = LED (current flows the way it points) · striped ⏚ = ground (any GND pin or the − rail) · flag = 3V3/5V supply · ○ = a Pi GPIO pin.
  • Breadboard: columns a b c d e (left half) / f g h i j (right half); holes in the same row & half are joined.
  • Extension board: left-column pins sit at hole e (use free holes a–d); right-column at hole f (use g–j). Find a pin by its printed label. Build rows ~21+ are empty.
